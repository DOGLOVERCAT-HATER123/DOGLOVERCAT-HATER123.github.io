Tips on how to gain and lose weight

First of all...

You must ask yourself first if you either want to lose weight or gain weight?

Gaining weight

To gain weight, you need to do a calorie surplus which is also known as a bulk, a calorie surplus meaning you take more calories than your usual daily intake, which means that you consume more higher calories than your body can burn, to start gaining weight or bulking you must first calculate your bodyweight in pounds and multiply it by 10 or just search for a calorie calculator in Google, there are 2 popular types of a bulk which are called clean bulk & dirty bulk, the clean bulk is where you eat clean while still being in a calorie surplus with their diet consisting of whole, unprocessed foods while avoiding processed foods high in fat and sugar, the other method being the dirty bulk is a period where you just eat any type of food which will result in an aggressive weight gain and increase in body fat percentage which can be harmful to your health if done for long periods of time, bulking is often last within 4-6 months because anymore than that will result in unnecessary weight gain.

Losing weight

To lose weight, you need to do a calorie deficit which is just basically calories in, calories out meaning you burn more calories than your daily calorie count, regardless of anything that you eat it just comes down to how many calories you consume, you can start by subtracting 100-200 calories from your daily intake, the problem with being in a calorie deficit is that you will become more easily exhausted and your energy levels will be overall lower and experience cravings, Again it's just personal preference, it just matters on how you are disciplined and if you will stick to it in the long term.
